Spokane Arts Academy is a K-5 charter school opening in Fall 2027 in Spokane, Washington. Our mission is to serve 150 students in our first year, growing to 350 students by Year 5 through a project-based arts-integrated curriculum.

Our instructional model centers on arts integration across core subjects. Each classroom is capped at 24 students with a full-time certificated teacher. We partner with local arts organizations for residencies in music, visual art, dance, and theater. A dedicated arts coordinator oversees these partnerships and ensures curricular alignment.

We plan to serve a diverse student body reflective of our neighborhood: approximately 40 percent free or reduced lunch, 12 percent with IEPs, and 10 percent English learners. Our staffing plan reflects these demographics with a dedicated SPED teacher, an English Learner specialist, and an instructional coach who focuses on differentiation.

Our financial plan assumes a 76 percent fill rate in Year 1 conservative target, scaling to 90 percent by Year 3 as community awareness builds. We have secured a 250,000 dollar CSP planning grant and are pursuing additional startup philanthropy from regional foundations focused on arts education.

Facilities planning is underway. We are in conversations with two potential landlords for a 15,000 square foot building within the district that supports our arts programming needs, including a dedicated black-box theater space and a large multipurpose room.
